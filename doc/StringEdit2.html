cost :: Fractional a => Char -> Char -> a

Calculate the cost of aligning two characters: 1. Vowel-to-vowel costs 0.5, 2. Vowel-to-consonant costs 1.2, 3. Consonant-to-consonant costs 0.6, and 4. Aligning the same characters costs 0.
